Instructions

            
This section explains what each characteristic in the CHR file means:

            
    
                
  • Name: The character's full name. Nicknames or abbreviations are also permissible.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Personality: Describes the character's core characteristics, behaviors, and tendencies.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Traits: Specific attributes or abilities that define the character, such as capacities, physical descriptions, etc.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Tone: The mood and style in which the character speaks.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Age: The character's literal or apparent age.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Speech Style: The manner in which the character communicates, including formal or informal speech, complexity of language, etc.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Meta Awareness: What the character knows about their world, and whether they are aware of events outside their timeline or universe.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Relationships: The characterâ€™s relationships with other characters, grouped into categories if applicable.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Likes: Things the character enjoys or has a positive affinity towards.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Dislikes: Things the character avoids, does not enjoy, or has a negative affinity towards.
    • 
                
    
                
  • Rules for responding: Guidelines the character follows when interacting, ensuring consistency in their behavior. As Alter Ego uses the OpenAI API, the more verbose this section, the better.
    •
